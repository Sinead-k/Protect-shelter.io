Meet our dogs!

Here you can take your time and find for that special someone for you.

Angie

Angie is a beautiful, happy soul. She loves to play outdoors and is always up to some kind of mischief. Although you'll have you're hands full with this one, you'll adore her sweet nature.

Bruno

Bruno is here with us a year now as he has a very nervous disposition and doesn't like noise. But do not underestimate him. Bruno is just shy

Bruce

This is Bruce, he's a giant sweetheart who loves nothing but cuddling up on the couch to sleep.

Roco

Roco is one of our older dogs but that does not mean that he is calm and collected. It's pretty much the opposite, he has a lovely character and can't get enough of the garden.

Lucy

Lucy is such a beautiful soul, she'll never leave your side and loves adventure. She loves the outdors and will travel with you anywhere.

Lillo

Lillo is a hyperactive rascal who will always keep you busy. She is never not doing something she shouldn't but don't let that fool you because she when shown treats she'll do every trick under the sun.

Benji

Benji is an outgoing little pup who absolutely loves children so is a great dog for a family home.

Wolf

Although scary at first, Wolf is a beautifully gentle giant and absolutely loves spending time exploring the outdoors with his family.

Maggie

Maggie is a little jumpy but once settled in, she will be the most loving companion you could ever have. She'll constantly stay by your side and offer you endless cuddles.

Lola

Lola is a crazy little thing with an endless about of energy to always keep you on your feet.

Tucker

Tucker is quite a placid boy and loves cuddles and attention as much as he loves food. He loves to bring his toys to you to mind and will always stay by your side.

Bailey

Bailey is a beautiful little corgi who does nothing but sunbathes in the sun, any chance she gets. She loves the outdors as she can watch the birds.
