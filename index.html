Welcome to Protect

Protect is an organisation whose goal is to rehome as many of our strays as we can.

There are a lot of dogs in Ireland that have just been abandoned by their owners without a clean place to stay or any food or water. This is a growing problem here in Ireland and we believe that every dog deserves a home. In 2014 the euthanasia figure was around 3,000. This figure would have been more than 9,000 without help from rescuers. Please join the fight and adopt a friend for life.

You can help us make the world a happier place by donating to a shelter near you!

Shelters are often non profit organisations with a sole goal of keeping as many dogs off the streets as possible. These shelters rescue dogs and cats who don't have a home anymore. Puppy farms are also a huge problem in Ireland as we have enough dogs on the streets as it is and these only add to those numbers.
